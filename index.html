WordQD. - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page outlines the types of data WordQD collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

WordQD does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers or third parties. All gameplay data, such as your daily word submission scores, is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults database.

Logging and Diagnostics

WordQD does not collect any crash logs, diagnostics, or analytics data. If you have opted in to share app analytics with Apple, crash data may be shared with Apple as part of their system, but this information is not accessible to us.

Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Deleting the app will permanently erase all stored data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at: wordqd_support@fastmail.com.
